Evidencing LLM Misuse

A Hands-on Forensic Tutorial on Copyright Infringement and Plagiarism Detection — built around Copyright Detective.

About this tutorial

This lecture-style KDD tutorial connects research on auditing LLM outputs with a hands-on forensic mindset: treating copyright infringement and plagiarism risks as an evidence discovery process—moving beyond single-score verdicts toward reproducible artifacts and structured reasoning.

The session is organized around interactive workflows inspired by Copyright Detective (content recall / similarity-style probes, iterative prompting, and reporting patterns suitable for review by practitioners and stakeholders).

Detailed outline, session schedule, slide deck, exercises, and companion links will be published here closer to the conference (organizer deadline for tutorial materials / website: July 1, 2026).
